Unit info

Unit strength is denoted on the piece. Movement range is one unless denoted otherwise.

The Important Thing: Game ends after this is captured. Immobile.

Mystic: Can see details about pieces directly adjacent.

Rider: Can move any number of spaces vertically or horizontally as long as it isn't through units/boulders.

Trap: Immobile, but destroys anything that attacks it besides engineers. The trap is also destroyed when something attacks it though.

Archer: Can shoot units 1 or 2 spaces away vertically/horizontally for 3 damage. This counts as a turn. Has strength 1 while defending.

Engineer: Can defuse trap

Assassin: Can defeat the commander if it attacks first

Soldier: No special abilities

Captain: No special abilities

Commander: No special abilities
